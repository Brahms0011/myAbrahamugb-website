About Me

I’m Abraham Ugbede Okeme, a dedicated Data Manager, M&E Specialist, and Technical Support Professional based in Lokoja, Kogi State. I have over six years of experience working in data management, health program monitoring, and ICT support across organizations such as Catholic Relief Services, Society for Family Health, and New Hope Agency International. I specialize in managing and reviewing health data using tools like Kobo Collect, ODK, NMRS, NOMIS, Red Rose OneApp, and Microsoft Excel, ensuring accuracy, confidentiality, and timely reporting. My background in Electrical and Electronic Engineering strengthens my ability to integrate technology with data-driven solutions to improve program outcomes.Beyond data, I’m also skilled in graphic design, digital systems setup, and training, combining creativity and technical precision to deliver results. I’m passionate about using technology to support public health, enhance data quality, and create efficient digital workflows.
